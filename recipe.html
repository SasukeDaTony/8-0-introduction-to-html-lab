Description

Tony's Perfect Bowl Of Cereal

Ingredients

  • Favorite Cereal
  • Favorite type of milk
  • 1 spoon
  • 1 bowl any size of your choosing

Directions

  1. take empty bowl side up
  2. pour favorite cereal into bowl until about to spill over
  3. pour favorite type of milk into bowl slowly and use spoon to push cereal down into milk and prevent spillage
